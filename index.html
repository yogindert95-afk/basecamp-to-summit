The method

Why we teach it as a climb.

A climb has a shape everyone understands: you start low, you commit to a route, the middle is steep, and the summit is earned — not given. Leadership works the same way.

So we don't hand people frameworks and wish them luck. Every session is half teaching, half practice on your real team, your real decisions — so it works the next morning, not just in the workshop.
